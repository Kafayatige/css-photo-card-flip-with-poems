Poems Snippets by Derrick Chidumebi

To the birds with the fear of heights
To those who watch their dreams chase them back to sleep when they dare to chase their dreams
To the free who call the cage home

To those torn between “Slow down” and “Go for it”
To those who dare not flap their wings lest their feathers fall Tell me,
what power possesses a bird to take flight? Of earth below or the sky above
I teach the waters that this chaos that drowns me from within isn't mine
I was born with this thing around my neck

But she would also tell me to love
Love like you’re breaking up tomorrow Knowing tomorrow may never come, but damn, heaven is blushing today
She would say, “remember why you left her That reason could be what brings you back home to yourselves
Some bridges need to be burnt but save some genuine ones

Rid yourself of hate
Be grateful to the universe for every moment spent
Be thankful for the journey of finding self br Love is like a painting Sometimes it is finished at the climax of its overwhelming beauty
Breakfast is the most important meal of the day
